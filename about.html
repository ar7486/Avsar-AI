About Us

Empowering India's Future Workforce

Our Internship Platform is a government initiative designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and professional experience. We connect talented students and young professionals with meaningful internship opportunities across India's diverse industries.

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality internship opportunities and enable every young Indian to unlock their potential through practical, hands-on experience in their chosen field.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for internships, fostering a skilled workforce that drives the nation's economic growth and innovation.

Pan-India Reach

Covering all 36 states and union territories with opportunities in 734+ districts
